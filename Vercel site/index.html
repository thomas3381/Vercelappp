How to Contact PayPal Customer Service

Need to reach PayPal support? Here's how you can contact them quickly!

Step 1: Call PayPal Customer Service

If you're having trouble with your PayPal account, the easiest way to get help is to contact their customer service team. You can reach them by calling:

+1 800-891-9140

Once you call, follow the prompts to reach the appropriate department for your issue.

Step 2: Use the PayPal App

If you're using the PayPal mobile app, you can access customer support directly through the app by following these steps:

  • Open the PayPal app
  • Go to the "Help & Contact" section
  • Select your issue or contact an agent
